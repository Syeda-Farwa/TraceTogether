About Trace Together

Our Promise

Never giving up on bringing families home

Our Mission: Reuniting Pakistan's Families

Since 2019, Trace Together has served as Pakistan's premier missing persons support organization. We combine advanced technology, professional expertise, and community solidarity to provide comprehensive assistance to families facing the unthinkable reality of a missing loved one.

Our Vision: A Connected Pakistan

We envision a Pakistan where sophisticated early warning systems, community networks, and rapid response protocols ensure that no family faces the crisis of a missing person without immediate, professional support and the full power of community behind them.

Why This Work Matters

Beyond statistics lies human suffering - parents searching for children, spouses seeking partners, children hoping for parents' return. Every case represents a family's worst nightmare and their greatest hope. Our multidisciplinary approach addresses not just the search, but the emotional, legal, and practical challenges families face during these critical times.

Specialized Services

Professional Investigation Services

Licensed private investigators, forensic specialists, and database analysts working with law enforcement agencies across Pakistan to locate missing persons using cutting-edge technology and proven methodologies.

Trauma-Informed Care

Licensed clinical psychologists, social workers, and peer counselors providing specialized support for secondary trauma, prolonged grief, and the unique psychological challenges of ambiguous loss.

Critical Response Protocol

Immediate deployment within 2 hours for high-risk cases including child abductions, elderly with dementia, and individuals with mental health crises. Coordinated with CPLC and provincial police forces.

Professional Investigation Services

Our team of licensed investigators uses advanced technology including facial recognition, database analysis, and coordinated efforts with law enforcement agencies across all provinces of Pakistan. We maintain a 87% success rate in locating missing persons within the first 72 hours.

Trauma-Informed Care

Our licensed clinicians specialize in the unique psychological challenges faced by families of missing persons. We offer individual counseling, family therapy, support groups, and 24/7 crisis intervention services.

Critical Response Protocol

Our rapid response team is specially trained for high-risk missing persons cases. We coordinate directly with CPLC, provincial police forces, and community organizations to ensure the fastest possible response.

Response Time: Deployment within 2 hours of report

Specializations:
