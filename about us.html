For the last 5 years, Trace Together has been Pakistan's beacon of hope for families facing the trauma of missing loved ones.

Our Journey Began with a Promise

Trace Together was founded in 2019 with a simple yet powerful mission: no family should face the trauma of a missing loved one alone. What started as a small initiative has grown into Pakistan's most comprehensive missing persons platform.

In collaboration with the Citizens Police Liaison Committee (CPLC) - Sindh, we've built a network of hope that spans across the nation.

Our Mission Statement

In partnership with the Citizens Police Liaison Committee (CPLC) - Sindh and communities across Pakistan, Trace Together is a nonprofit organization committed to helping locate and reunite missing family members. Our comprehensive platform provides families with the resources, community support, and advanced technology they need during their most challenging times. We are the bridge between despair and hope, between loss and reunion.

How We Make a Difference

Advanced Search Technology

Our proprietary matching algorithms analyze patterns, locations, and characteristics to identify potential matches faster than traditional methods, dramatically reducing search time.

24/7 Family Support

Our trained counselors and support specialists are available around the clock to provide emotional support, guidance, and practical assistance to families in crisis.

Our Impact Stories

Bringing Children Home

Over 1,200 missing children have been safely returned to their families through our coordinated efforts with law enforcement and community volunteers.

Mental Health Support

Our counseling services have provided psychological support to over 3,500 families, helping them cope with trauma and stress.

Prevention Education

We've conducted awareness programs in over 200 schools and communities, educating families about safety measures and prevention strategies.

Technology & Innovation

Pioneering Digital Solutions

At Trace Together, we leverage cutting-edge technology to revolutionize missing person search efforts in Pakistan. Our platform represents the most advanced missing persons database in South Asia.

AI-Powered Matching

Machine learning algorithms analyze facial features, physical characteristics, and behavioral patterns to suggest potential matches.

Mobile App Platform

Real-time reporting and alert system accessible through our mobile application, reaching communities across Pakistan instantly.

Secure Database

Bank-level security protecting sensitive information while enabling authorized personnel to access critical data quickly.
