Partnership Timeline

2019

Foundation Partnerships

Established initial partnerships with Edhi Foundation and CPLC for emergency response coordination.

2020

Child Protection Network

Expanded collaboration with Sahil and Save the Children to create specialized child protection protocols and training programs.

2021

Women's Safety Initiative

Partnered with Aurat Foundation and Women in Struggle for Empowerment (WISE) to address gender-based violence and missing women cases.

2022

Community Welfare Alliance

Joined forces with Saylani Welfare Trust and JDC Foundation to provide comprehensive family support services and community outreach.

2023

National Network Expansion

Established partnerships with 15 additional NGOs across all provinces, creating Pakistan's largest missing persons support network.

2024

Technology Integration

Launched integrated digital platform connecting all partner NGOs for real-time case sharing and coordinated response efforts.
